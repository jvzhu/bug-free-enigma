# ⏰ Launch Day Schedule — Bug-Free Enigma

Hour-by-hour execution timeline for ProductHunt launch day.
All times in **PST (Pacific Standard Time)** — adjust for your timezone.

> **PST conversions:** PST+3h = EST | PST+6h = UTC | PST+8h = CET | PST+16h = SGT

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If ranked below #10 at 8 AM PST
- Send personal DMs to your developer network (5–10 people)
- Post in 2–3 Slack/Discord communities (privacy, devs, open-source)
- Ask 3 friends to share the link on their personal social accounts
- Post a "last chance to support" tweet

### If app has a critical bug on launch day
```
[Post immediately in PH comments]

We're aware of [specific bug] and have a fix deploying now. GitHub Pages
auto-deploys within 2 minutes of merging. ETA: fixed in ~5 minutes.

Thank you to everyone who reported this. 🙏
```

### If receiving negative or critical comments
- Respond quickly, professionally, and thank them for feedback
- Acknowledge the criticism without arguing
- Example: "You're right that [X] is a limitation — here's why we made that
  trade-off and here's what we're doing about it."
- Never delete negative comments
