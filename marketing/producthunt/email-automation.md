## SUBJECT LINE TESTING GUIDE

Test these variables for best open rates:

| Variable | Option A | Option B | Option C |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Emoji in subject | ✅ Emoji | ❌ No emoji | — |
| Personalization | "[NAME]," opener | Generic "Hi," | No greeting |
| Urgency | "48-hour offer" | "Limited spots" | No urgency |
| Numbers | "[N] upvotes" | "Top #[N]" | "100+ users" |
| CTA tone | "Support us" | "Check it out" | "Share feedback" |

**Best practice:** Send Email 1 to 50% of list with Subject A, 50% with Subject B.
Use the higher-open-rate subject for all subsequent re-sends.
